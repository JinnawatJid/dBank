# dBank Premium Credit Card Policy

This document outlines the features, fees, and eligibility criteria for the dBank Premium Credit Card product.

## Overview
The dBank Premium Credit Card is designed for high-net-worth individuals who travel frequently and desire premium concierge services, comprehensive travel insurance, and accelerated rewards points.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Minimum Age:** 21 years old.
- **Minimum Income:** $150,000 annual gross income (verified via recent tax returns or pay stubs).
- **Credit Score:** Excellent credit history (FICO score of 740 or higher).
- **Existing Relationship:** Applicants must have an active dBank checking or savings account in good standing for at least 6 months.

## Fees and Interest Rates
- **Annual Fee:** $495 (Not waived for the first year).
- **Additional Cardholder Fee:** $75 per authorized user.
- **Foreign Transaction Fee:** 0% (No foreign transaction fees).
- **Standard APR:** 18.99% - 24.99% Variable, depending on creditworthiness.
- **Late Payment Fee:** Up to $40.

## Rewards Program
- **Travel & Dining:** Earn 3x points on all travel (airlines, hotels, car rentals) and dining purchases globally.
- **Other Purchases:** Earn 1x point on all other eligible purchases.
- **Sign-up Bonus:** 50,000 bonus points after spending $4,000 on purchases in the first 3 months from account opening.

## Premium Benefits
- **Airport Lounge Access:** Complimentary Priority Pass Select membership.
- **Travel Insurance:** Includes trip cancellation/interruption insurance, baggage delay insurance, and secondary rental car collision damage waiver.
- **Concierge Service:** 24/7 access to the dBank Premium Concierge for dining reservations, event tickets, and travel planning.
