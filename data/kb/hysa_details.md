# High-Yield Savings Account Details

Information regarding the dBank High-Yield Savings Account (HYSA).

## Account Overview
The dBank High-Yield Savings Account is designed to help customers grow their wealth faster with highly competitive interest rates compared to traditional savings accounts, all with zero monthly maintenance fees.

## Interest Rates and APY
- **Current APY:** 4.25% Annual Percentage Yield (APY) as of October 1st, 2023.
- **Variable Rate:** The interest rate and APY are variable and subject to change at any time without notice, based on market conditions and Federal Reserve rate adjustments.
- **Compounding:** Interest is compounded daily and credited to the account monthly on the last business day of the statement cycle.
- **No Tiering:** The 4.25% APY applies to the entire balance; there are no minimum balance tiers required to earn the highest rate.

## Fees and Minimums
- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $0
- **Minimum Opening Deposit:** $100
- **Minimum Balance Requirement:** $0 (No minimum daily balance required to keep the account open or avoid fees).

## Transaction Limits (Regulation D)
- Historically, federal Regulation D limited certain types of withdrawals and transfers from savings accounts to a total of six (6) per monthly statement cycle.
- **Current Status:** The Federal Reserve has paused the enforcement of the 6-withdrawal limit. However, dBank reserves the right to impose a $10 fee for every withdrawal or transfer exceeding six per month if the policy is reinstated.
- Unlimited withdrawals are allowed if made in person at a branch, by mail, or at an ATM.

## Funding the Account
Customers can fund their HYSA through:
- Internal transfers from a dBank checking account.
- Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT/ACH) from external linked bank accounts.
- Mobile check deposit.
- Wire transfers (standard incoming wire fees apply).
